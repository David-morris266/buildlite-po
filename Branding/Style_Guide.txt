BuildLite Brand Style Guide

Colours:
- Neon Green: #39FF14
- Teal: #00BFA6
- Dark Background: #0A0A0A
- White: #FFFFFF
- Grey: #CCCCCC

Typography:
- Logo font: Bold sans-serif (suggested: Montserrat, Poppins, or Open Sans)
- Body font: Clean sans-serif (Roboto, Lato, or Open Sans)

Usage:
- Full logo for splash/login screens and documents
- Compact logo for navbars/headers
- Icon for favicon and app icons
- Use dark background version for UI, light version for documents/PDFs
